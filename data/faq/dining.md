## Q: What dining venues are available at the club?

A: We offer three distinctive dining experiences:
- **Coral Room & Longtail Terrace**: Main dining room and oceanview terrace
- **Beach Terrace**: Casual beachside dining (seasonal)
- **Frozen Hut**: Beach bar with frozen drinks and light fare (seasonal)

## Q: Do I need reservations for dinner?

A: Yes, dinner reservations are highly recommended, especially during peak season and for Thursday/Saturday formal nights. Contact the front desk or use the "Dining Reservation" button in this app.

## Q: What is the dress code for dining?

A: 
- **Coral Room (Dinner)**: Resort elegant; gentlemen require jackets on Thursday and Saturday evenings
- **Lunch/Breakfast**: Smart casual resort wear
- **Beach Terrace & Frozen Hut**: Casual beachwear acceptable
- **Note**: No denim, athletic wear, or baseball caps in the Coral Room

## Q: Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

A: Absolutely! Our chefs can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary needs. Please inform your server or notify us when making reservations.

## Q: What are the dining hours?

A: 
- **Breakfast**: 7:00 AM - 10:30 AM (Coral Room)
- **Lunch**: 12:00 PM - 2:30 PM (varies by venue)
- **Dinner**: 6:30 PM - 9:00 PM (Coral Room)
- **Beach Terrace**: Seasonal hours, typically 12:00 PM - 4:00 PM
- **Frozen Hut**: Seasonal, typically 11:00 AM - sunset

## Q: Is room service available?

A: Limited room service is available for breakfast and light meals. Contact the front desk to place an order. Full room service may be available for cottages.

## Q: Can non-members dine at the club?

A: Non-members may dine when accompanied by a member or as registered hotel guests. Some restrictions may apply during peak times.

## Q: Do you offer children's menus?

A: Yes, children's menus are available at all dining venues. High chairs and booster seats are also available upon request.

## Q: What about special occasion dining?

A: We love celebrating special occasions! Notify us when making reservations, and we can arrange special touches like custom cakes, champagne, or preferred seating. Additional charges may apply.

## Q: Is outside food allowed?

A: Outside food and beverages are generally not permitted in dining areas. However, special arrangements can be made for dietary or medical needs.

## Q: Do you serve alcohol?

A: Yes, we have a full bar service offering wines, cocktails, and local and international beers. The Frozen Hut specializes in tropical frozen drinks.

## Q: Can I charge meals to my room?

A: Yes, registered hotel guests can charge dining to their room account. Members can charge to their member accounts.

## Q: Do you offer themed dining nights?

A: Yes! We feature special dining events including:
- Thursday Barbecue Buffet (seasonal)
- Saturday Traditional Bermuda Codfish Breakfast
- Sunday Brunch
- Special holiday menus

## Q: What if I want to dine outside the club?

A: Bermuda offers excellent restaurants! The front desk can provide recommendations and make reservations at popular local establishments. Remember to book taxis in advance for evening dining.
