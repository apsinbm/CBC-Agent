## Q: What wedding venues are available?

A: We offer 8 stunning venues:
- **Beaches**: Coral Beach Bluff (150 guests), Breakers Beach (75), Wedding Beach (150)
- **Lawns**: Wedding Lawn (400), Club Lawn (250), Wheelhouse Lawn (100)
- **Indoor**: Club Room (125), Longtail Terrace (100)

## Q: What are the venue rental fees?

A: Site fees vary by venue and event size. Beach venues typically range from $1,000-$3,000. Please contact our events team for specific pricing based on your needs.

## Q: Do you have rain backup options?

A: Yes! Every outdoor venue has a designated indoor backup:
- Beach ceremonies → Longtail Terrace
- Lawn receptions → Club Room
- We monitor weather closely and can transition seamlessly if needed

## Q: What catering options are available?

A: We offer flexible catering from casual to formal:
- Buffet service
- Plated dinners
- Cocktail receptions
- Custom menus available
- Bar service on consumption basis

## Q: Can we have a beach ceremony?

A: Absolutely! We have three beach venues. Ceremonies typically occur 2-3 hours before sunset for the best lighting. We provide setup including chairs, arch, and aisle runner.

## Q: What's included in wedding packages?

A: Basic packages typically include:
- Venue rental
- Setup and breakdown
- White folding chairs
- Standard linens
- Dance floor (if needed)
- Dedicated event coordinator

## Q: How far in advance should we book?

A: We recommend booking 9-12 months in advance, especially for peak season (May-October) and popular dates. Member events receive priority booking.

## Q: Can we bring our own vendors?

A: We have a preferred vendor list covering photography, flowers, music, and more. Outside vendors may be permitted with approval and may require additional fees.

## Q: What about accommodations for wedding guests?

A: We can arrange room blocks for wedding parties. With approximately 45 accommodations including cottages and suites, we can host intimate destination weddings. Contact reservations for group rates.

## Q: Do you handle legal requirements?

A: We can guide you through Bermuda's marriage requirements:
- Notice of Intended Marriage must be published
- Application required at least 15 days before
- Our coordinator can assist with the process

## Q: Can we have a rehearsal dinner?

A: Yes! Many couples host rehearsal dinners at our Beach Terrace or Coral Room. We can create custom menus for your pre-wedding events.

## Q: What about wedding photography?

A: Our scenic property offers countless photo opportunities. Popular spots include:
- The beach at sunset
- Historic coral stone walls
- Tropical gardens
- Ocean bluff overlooks

## Q: Is there a wedding coordinator?

A: Yes, our dedicated events team will work with you from planning through execution. They'll coordinate with all vendors and ensure your day runs smoothly.

## Q: Can we have fireworks?

A: Fireworks require special permits in Bermuda. We can assist with the application process, though approval isn't guaranteed. Consider sparkler send-offs as an alternative.

## Q: What's the capacity for different event types?

A: 
- Ceremony only: Up to 400 guests (Wedding Lawn)
- Cocktail reception: Up to 250 guests
- Seated dinner: Up to 150 guests (venue dependent)
- Dancing reception: Consider space needed for band/DJ and dance floor

## Q: Do you accommodate special dietary needs?

A: Yes! Our culinary team can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, kosher, and other dietary requirements. Discuss needs during menu planning.

## Q: What time do events end?

A: Outdoor events typically end by 10:00 PM due to noise considerations. Indoor receptions may continue later. Discuss timing during planning.

## Q: Can we have a wedding brunch?

A: Absolutely! Post-wedding brunches are popular, especially our Sunday Brunch. We can arrange private or semi-private dining areas.
