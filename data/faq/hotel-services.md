## Q: What are the front desk hours?

A: The front desk is open from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily. You can reach them at +1 (441) 236-2233. From your room, simply dial "206".

## Q: What are check-in and check-out times?

A: Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability. Contact Reception to arrange.

## Q: Is breakfast included with my stay?

A: Yes! All accommodation rates include daily breakfast served in the Coral Room. Breakfast hours are typically 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM.

## Q: Do you offer laundry service?

A: Yes, laundry and dry cleaning services are available. Place items in the laundry bag in your room and contact housekeeping. Same-day service is available for items received before 9:00 AM (additional charges apply).

## Q: Is Wi-Fi available?

A: Complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the property. Connect to "CoralBeach_Guest" network. Contact the front desk if you need assistance with the password.

## Q: What about housekeeping service?

A: Daily housekeeping service is provided for all accommodations. If you prefer not to be disturbed, please use the privacy sign on your door. For additional housekeeping requests, contact the front desk.

## Q: Is the club wheelchair accessible?

A: The main clubhouse and many facilities are accessible. Some of our historic cottages may have limited accessibility due to their original architecture. Please contact reservations@coralbeach.bm to discuss specific accessibility needs.

## Q: Do you have a lost and found?

A: Yes, the lost and found is managed by the front desk. If you've misplaced something during your stay, please contact them at +1 (441) 236-2233.

## Q: Can you store luggage?

A: Yes, we offer complimentary luggage storage for early arrivals and late departures. The front desk will securely store your bags so you can enjoy club facilities while waiting.

## Q: Are there safety deposit boxes?

A: In-room safes are available in most accommodations. For additional security needs or larger valuables, please inquire at the front desk.

## Q: Do you offer wake-up calls?

A: Yes, wake-up calls can be arranged through the front desk. Dial "206" from your room to schedule.

## Q: What if I need medical assistance?

A: For medical emergencies, dial 911. For non-emergencies, the front desk can assist with contacting local medical services or pharmacies. King Edward VII Memorial Hospital is the island's main hospital.

## Q: Can you arrange special room amenities?

A: Yes! We can arrange special amenities like flowers, champagne, fruit baskets, or birthday cakes. Please contact the front desk at least 24 hours in advance to arrange.
