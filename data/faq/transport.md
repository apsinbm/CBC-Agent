## Q: Do you arrange airport transfers?

A: Yes! Reception can arrange taxis or private cars for your transfer to/from L.F. Wade International Airport. Typical taxi time is approximately 25 minutes. The cost is around $80 each way including service. Please arrange in advance, especially if arriving late.

## Q: How do I book a taxi during my stay?

A: Book taxis through the front desk. For peak times (dinner reservations, special events), we recommend scheduling a day ahead to ensure availability. The front desk can be reached at +1 (441) 236-2233.

## Q: How long does it take to get to the club from the airport?

A: The drive from L.F. Wade International Airport to Coral Beach & Tennis Club typically takes 25-30 minutes by taxi, depending on traffic conditions.

## Q: Is there parking at the club?

A: Yes, complimentary parking is available for members and guests. Our valet service is also available during peak times and special events.

## Q: Can I rent a scooter or car in Bermuda?

A: Yes, scooter and car rentals are available on the island. The front desk can assist with arrangements. Note that Bermuda drives on the left side of the road, and visitors need a temporary driving permit. Scooters are a popular way to explore the island.

## Q: Are there public buses to the club?

A: Bermuda has a public bus system, and there are stops along South Road near the club. Ask Reception for the current bus schedule and the nearest stop location. Exact change is required for bus fare.

## Q: What about ferry service?

A: Bermuda's ferry system connects various parts of the island. While there isn't a ferry terminal at the club, ferries can be a scenic way to explore. The front desk can provide ferry schedules and suggest routes for sightseeing.

## Q: Should I arrange transportation in advance for dinner reservations?

A: Yes, especially during peak season or for popular restaurants. We recommend booking taxis through the front desk at least a day in advance for evening dining reservations outside the club.

## Q: Are ride-sharing services available in Bermuda?

A: Ride-sharing apps like Uber and Lyft are not currently available in Bermuda. Taxis are the primary option for transportation, and our front desk is happy to arrange them for you.

## Q: How much should I tip taxi drivers?

A: The standard tip for taxi drivers in Bermuda is 15-20% of the fare. Many visitors round up to the nearest $5 or $10 for convenience.
