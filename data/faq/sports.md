## Q: What is the tennis dress code?

A: Tennis requires 90% white or cream-colored clothing, maintaining our traditional standards. Clay court soles or non-aggressive sole treads are required on our Har-Tru courts - no aggressive treads allowed.

## Q: How many tennis courts do you have?

A: We have 8 championship Har-Tru clay courts, with 4 courts lit for evening play. Courts must be reserved in advance.

## Q: Do you have pickleball?

A: Yes! We have 4 hard courts dedicated to pickleball. Any respectful sports attire is acceptable. Equipment is available at the Tennis Shop.

## Q: What about squash courts?

A: We have 2 international-standard squash courts. Any respectful sports attire is acceptable, but non-marking soles are required. Court keys can be rented annually for members.

## Q: What equipment is available at the Tennis Shop?

A: The Tennis Shop carries Babolat, Sofibella, and Nike brands. We stock racquets, balls, shoes, and attire for tennis, pickleball, and squash. Rental equipment is also available.

## Q: Do you offer tennis lessons?

A: Yes! We offer private lessons, group clinics, junior development programs, and adult strategy sessions year-round. Contact the Tennis Shop to schedule.

## Q: What are the gym hours?

A: The fitness center is open 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily, featuring cardio and strength-training equipment. Members have 24-hour access with their key card.

## Q: Are fitness classes available?

A: Yes! We offer:
- Morning yoga on the lawn (9:00-10:00 AM)
- Group cardio classes
- HIIT sessions
- Personal training (by appointment)

## Q: Can I play croquet?

A: Yes! We have a croquet lawn available for complimentary use. Equipment and bookings are available through the front desk.

## Q: Is there a putting green?

A: Yes, we have a putting green open from sunrise to sunset. Putters and balls are available - ask at the front desk or Tennis Shop.

## Q: What beach sports are available?

A: Beach activities include:
- Beach volleyball
- Beach tennis
- Bocce ball
- Snorkeling (gear available)

## Q: Do members get priority for court bookings?

A: Yes, members receive priority booking for all courts and can reserve further in advance. Members should provide their member number when booking.

## Q: Are there fees for court usage?

A: Court fees vary by sport and member status. Members typically have complimentary or reduced rates. Guests may incur fees - please check when booking.

## Q: Can I rent equipment?

A: Yes, rental equipment is available for all racquet sports through the Tennis Shop. Beach sports equipment is available at the front desk.

## Q: What other activities are available?

A: Additional activities include:
- Basketball practice court
- Games room with billiards and table tennis
- Beach activities and water sports
- Walking paths through our 26-acre gardens

## Q: Do you host tournaments?

A: Yes! We host regular tennis, pickleball, and squash tournaments for members, plus special events throughout the year. Check with the Tennis Shop for the current schedule.
