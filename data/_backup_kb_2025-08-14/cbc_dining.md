# Dining at Coral Beach & Tennis Club

The Coral Beach & Tennis Club offers three incredible restaurants, all with exceptional ocean views. From relaxed beachside lunches served to your lounger to elegant dinners on Longtail Terrace, there's an option for every occasion.

## Overview

- **The Coral Room & Longtail Terrace** — Chef-driven, original, sophisticated dishes in an elegant setting with sweeping South Shore views. Ideal for refined dinners or special evenings.
- **The Beach Terrace** — Casual favorite for beachgoers; relaxed dress, simple and satisfying menu, ocean breezes, and a laid-back atmosphere.
- **The Frozen Hut** — Order lunchtime fare and refreshing cocktails from your beach chair, or dine on the oceanside deck. Great for light bites and drinks by the water.

## Venue Details

### The Coral Room & Longtail Terrace
- **Vibe**: Elegant, refined, special-occasion friendly
- **Food**: Chef's original, sophisticated dishes
- **Setting**: Longtail Terrace features panoramic ocean views
- **Good for**: Romantic dinners, anniversaries, celebratory meals

### The Beach Terrace
- **Vibe**: Relaxed, casual, beach-friendly
- **Food**: Simple, fresh menu; easygoing service
- **Setting**: Terrace overlooking pink sand and turquoise waters
- **Good for**: Come-as-you-are beach days, casual lunches

### The Frozen Hut (and Deck)
- **Vibe**: Lively beach bar and grill
- **Food & Drink**: Lunchtime favorites, beach BBQs, tropical cocktails
- **Setting**: Large beachside deck steps from the sand; ample space for buffet or live music events
- **Service**: Order from your beach chair or dine on the deck
- **Good for**: Midday meals, drinks with a view, beach days

## Event & Private Spaces (Dining-Adjacent Options)

These spaces can complement dining needs for private events:

## Dining Q&A Reference

### Common Questions & Answers

**Q: Where can I eat at the Club?**
A: We have three restaurants: The Coral Room & Longtail Terrace (elegant dining), The Beach Terrace (casual beachside), and The Frozen Hut (beach bar with deck). All offer ocean views. Would you like help with a reservation?

**Q: What is the Frozen Hut?**
A: The Frozen Hut is our lively beach bar and grill serving lunch favorites and tropical cocktails. You can order from your beach chair or dine on the oceanside deck. Perfect for casual midday dining with your toes in the sand.

**Q: Which restaurant is best for a romantic dinner?**
A: The Coral Room & Longtail Terrace is perfect for romantic dinners, featuring chef-driven sophisticated dishes with panoramic ocean views from the terrace.

**Q: Can I get lunch on the beach?**
A: Absolutely! The Frozen Hut serves lunch and drinks right to your beach chair, or you can dine on their deck. The Beach Terrace also offers casual beachside dining.

**Q: Do you have a private dining room?**
A: Yes, The Cave is our intimate wine cellar perfect for private dinners. We also have the Main Lounge, Frozen Hut Deck, and Wedding Lawn for larger private events.

**Q: What's the dress code for dinner?**
A: Smart casual at The Coral Room & Longtail Terrace (jackets required for gentlemen on Thursday & Saturday evenings). The Beach Terrace is relaxed and beach-friendly.

**Q: How do I book dinner?**
A: You can use the Dining Reservation option in the chatbot or contact Reception directly. We'll confirm your preferred date, time, and party size.
