# Venues & Events at Coral Beach & Tennis Club

## Overview

The Coral Beach & Tennis Club's 26 acres offer some of Bermuda's most exclusive and breathtaking event venues. From the iconic Longtail Terrace to the expansive Wedding Lawn, there's a location to suit any event type and group size — from 5 to 300 guests.

The Club is one of Bermuda's most sought-after wedding destinations, featured in Bermudian Weddings, Town & Country, the Huffington Post, and Brides.com. Our event planning staff guide you through every detail to make your dream wedding or private event a reality.

## Event Types We Host
- Weddings & vow renewals
- Corporate events & holiday parties  
- Birthday celebrations & anniversaries
- Family gatherings & beach bonfires
- Private sports tournaments or socials

## Key Venues

### Longtail Terrace
- Elegant, iconic setting with sweeping ocean views
- Ideal for ceremonies, receptions, or upscale dinners

### Wedding Lawn
- Manicured outdoor lawn nestled among cottages
- Accommodates up to ~400 guests for ceremonies or receptions
- Perfect for large gatherings under the Bermuda sky

### Main Lounge
- Cozy clubhouse heart with ocean views, fireplace, and grand piano
- Suitable for cocktail receptions or intimate gatherings

### The Cave
- Intimate wine cellar dining space
- Perfect for private dinners or small business meetings

### Frozen Hut Deck
- Large beachside deck just steps from the sand
- Ideal for BBQs, buffets, and live music

### Boardroom
- Functional meeting room with A/V equipment and buffet space
- Suitable for corporate meetings, presentations, and working lunches

### Sports Venues
- **Tennis courts** (8 clay) — reserve for socials or tournaments
- **Squash courts** (2) — host a squash-themed event
- **Pool** — great for children's pool parties
- **Croquet lawn** — host a croquet tournament
- **Putting green** — casual or competitive putting events
- Sports staff can set up equipment; F&B can provide refreshments

## Venues & Events Q&A Reference

### Common Questions & Answers

**Q: Where's the best place for a wedding?**
A: The Wedding Lawn accommodates up to 400 guests for ceremonies under the Bermuda sky, while Longtail Terrace offers an elegant setting with ocean views. We're featured in top wedding publications and offer bespoke planning. Would you like to see our exclusive wedding package?

**Q: Do you have meeting rooms?**
A: Yes, the Boardroom features A/V equipment and buffet space for corporate meetings. The Cave offers an intimate wine cellar setting for small business dinners or meetings.

**Q: Can I host a beach BBQ?**
A: Absolutely! The Frozen Hut Deck is perfect for beach BBQs, just steps from the sand with space for buffets and live music.

**Q: Do you have sports facilities for events?**
A: Yes — you can reserve our tennis courts, squash courts, croquet lawn, pool, or putting green for private events. Our sports staff will set up equipment and F&B can provide refreshments.

**Q: Can you provide catering?**
A: Yes, we offer full on-site catering including hors d'oeuvres, specialty pastries, custom menus, and wines from our temperature-controlled cellars, plus entertainment and decorating services.

**Q: What's the maximum guest capacity?**
A: Most events can accommodate up to 300 guests, with the Wedding Lawn hosting up to 400 for outdoor ceremonies or receptions.
