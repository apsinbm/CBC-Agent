# Activities at Coral Beach & Tennis Club

Our 26-acre oceanfront property offers a wide range of activities for members and guests—from beach days and water fun to racquet sports, fitness, gardens, and family games.

## Beach & Swimming
- One of Bermuda's finest pink-sand beaches; private and serviced in season
- **Service hours** (in season): Beach service from 10:00 AM; Frozen Hut Bar from 11:00 AM
- **Available activities**: beach volleyball, beach tennis, snorkeling, children's play area
- **Beach Attendants** provide loungers, umbrellas, side tables, and food & beverage service

## Golfing
- On-property 18-hole putting green available to members
- Access to multiple Bermuda golf courses via Front Desk assistance
- The Front Desk can book tee times at partner courses, often at special member rates

## Squash
- Two state-of-the-art international squash courts for members
- Regular socials and tournaments throughout the year
- Court keys may be rented on an annual basis at the Front Desk

## Croquet & Bocce Ball
- Croquet lawn and Bocce Ball court maintained for complimentary enjoyment
- Croquet equipment/bookings via the Front Desk
- Bocce equipment available at the Front Desk; court overlooks Tennis Court #7

## Games Room
- Board games (e.g., backgammon, chess), large flat-screen TV with cable
- Computer and printer for guest use
- Suitable for family movie nights or casual game nights

## Gardens & Walking
- Grounds feature native Bermuda flora and international species
- Cliff walk along one of Bermuda's last unspoiled coastlines
- Alfred Blackburn Smith Nature Reserve adjacent on the west end of the property
- **Garden Walking Tours** daily at 10:00 AM, led by professional landscapers

## Shopping
- **Beach Office**: Sun-day essentials (e.g., snorkeling gear, underwater cameras) and CBC-branded apparel
- **The Boutique**: Clothing, accessories, and items by local Bermudian artisans
- **The Island Shop by Barbara Finsness**: Colorful home décor and custom CBC pieces
- **Tennis Shop**: CBC-branded athletic wear and equipment for tennis/squash

## Fitness
- Fitness center with cardio and strength-training equipment; 24-hour access for members
- Weekly classes: outdoor yoga, HIIT, and more
- Personal training available via the fitness staff

## Booking & Info
- **Beach service, equipment, and amenities**: Beach Attendants (in season)
- **Squash/croquet/bocce bookings & equipment**: Front Desk
- **Tee times at partner golf courses**: Front Desk
- **Fitness classes and personal training**: Fitness staff

## Activities Q&A Reference

### Common Questions & Answers

**Q: What time does beach service start?**
A: Beach service begins at 10:00 AM (in season), with the Frozen Hut Bar opening at 11:00 AM.

**Q: Do you have squash courts?**
A: Yes — we have two international squash courts with regular socials and tournaments throughout the year. Court keys can be rented annually at the Front Desk.

**Q: Can we play croquet?**
A: Yes — we have a complimentary croquet lawn. Equipment and bookings are available through the Front Desk.

**Q: Is there a putting green?**
A: Yes — we have an 18-hole putting green on property for members. The Front Desk can also book tee times at partner golf courses around the island, often with special member rates.

**Q: Any garden tours?**
A: Yes — daily Garden Walking Tours at 10:00 AM, led by our professional landscapers.

**Q: Where can I buy CBC gear?**
A: We have four shopping locations: Beach Office, The Boutique, The Island Shop by Barbara Finsness, and the Tennis Shop.

**Q: Do you offer fitness classes?**
A: Yes — we offer weekly classes including outdoor yoga and HIIT, plus personal training. Members have 24/7 access to our fitness center.
