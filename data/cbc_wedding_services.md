## Terms & Conditions

### Payment
- Master account must be guaranteed by CBC member sponsor
- Credit cards incur 5% processing fee
- All prices subject to 17% gratuity

### Deposits & Payments
- Non-refundable deposit equal to site fee required upon booking
- 50% of estimated total due 90 days before event
- Final balance due 30 days before event

### Cancellation Policy
- No penalty for hurricane within 200 miles/72 hours of event
- Food costs and non-refundable vendor fees apply for other cancellations

### Food & Beverage
- All food and beverage must be provided by the club
- Final guest count required 2 weeks before event
- 17% gratuity added to all food and beverage

### Music & Entertainment
- Music must end by midnight unless special approval obtained

### Weather Policy
- Club management decides on indoor move for inclement weather
- Decision made day of event for guest safety and comfort

## Wedding Q&A Reference

### Common Questions & Answers

**Q: What's the largest wedding you can accommodate?**
A: The Wedding Lawn can host up to 400 guests for ceremonies and receptions. The Beach can accommodate up to 2,000 for large receptions.

**Q: Do you have indoor backup options?**
A: Yes, The Coral Room and Longtail Terrace provide elegant indoor alternatives. The club decides on weather-related moves for guest safety.

**Q: Can we bring our own caterer?**
A: All food and beverage must be provided by the club's culinary team to ensure quality and service standards.

**Q: What's included in the site fee?**
A: Site fees cover venue rental. Setup/breakdown, catering, bar service, and other services are additional.

**Q: Do you offer wedding packages?**
A: We offer customized wedding planning with various menu options and services. Contact Valerie Mesto for detailed package information.

**Q: Is there member pricing?**
A: Weddings must be sponsored by a CBC member. Contact us for member benefits and requirements.

**Q: What about accommodations for guests?**
A: We have cottages, suites, and 31 guest rooms available. Members receive priority booking.
