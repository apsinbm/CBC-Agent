# Tennis & Pickleball — Coral Beach & Tennis Club

## Overview
- **Tennis**: 8 championship Har-Tru clay courts, daily maintenance, new fencing, added shade areas
- **Pickleball**: 4 hard courts
- Year-round events (clinics, socials, tournaments) for members and guests

## History Note (Heritage)
- Bermuda native Mary Ewing Outerbridge introduced lawn tennis to the U.S. in 1875 (first U.S. court at Staten Island Cricket & Baseball Club)
- Two Bermuda clubs, including Coral Beach & Tennis Club, are written into the USTA constitution with voting rights; CBTC has collaborated with USTA and other federations to host significant tournaments

## Court Policies (Tennis)
- **Check-in**: All players must check in at the Tennis Pro Shop before playing
- **Reservations**: Required; play on the court assigned by the shop. Reservations may be made one week in advance
- **Guest policy**: All guests must be registered; $15 per guest
- **Attire**: Tennis requires white or cream (≈90% white) apparel and appropriate tennis footwear with white soles; clay-appropriate soles; no aggressive tread
- **Court care**: Sweeping after play is optional but appreciated
- **Lighting**: 4 courts available after dark with lights
- **Court counts**: Tennis 8, Pickleball 4

## Court Policies (Pickleball)
- All players must be listed on the reservation prior to play
- Guests must be listed (via app or by contacting the shop)
- **Guest fee**: $10 per guest; supports court maintenance/improvements
- **One-court rule**: One court per reservation
- **Duration**: Up to 120 minutes per reservation
- **Membership/registration**: Only registered members may use courts; failure to register guests may result in forfeiture

## Tennis & Pickleball Q&A Reference

### Common Questions & Answers

**Q: How many tennis courts do you have?**
A: We have 8 Har-Tru clay courts with daily maintenance. 4 courts have lights for evening play.

**Q: What's the tennis dress code?**
A: Tennis requires approximately 90% white or cream attire with clay-appropriate soles (white soles, no aggressive tread). Other racquet sports allow general sports attire.

**Q: Can I bring a guest to play?**
A: Yes, guests must be registered at the Tennis Shop. Guest fees are $15 for tennis and $10 for pickleball.

**Q: How do I book a court?**
A: Reserve through the Tennis Shop up to one week in advance. List all players on the reservation. For pickleball, one court per reservation for up to 120 minutes.

**Q: What are the Tennis Shop hours and contact?**
A: Open Mon–Fri 8:30 AM–4:00 PM, Sat 9:00 AM–12:00 PM, Sun closed. Phone: 239-7216, Email: tennis@coralbeach.bm.

**Q: Do you offer adult clinics?**
A: Yes — we offer Cardio Tennis, Live Ball, Doubles Workshop, Adult Start, and Tennis 101. Most drop-in clinics are $30.

**Q: What junior programs are available?**
A: We have programs for ages 4–10+ including Pre-Rally, Rising Stars, Orange Ballers, Junior Aces, Red Ball I, and Green Ballers. Drop-in rates range from $20–$35.

**Q: What are private lesson rates?**
A: Head Pro: $110/60min or $65/30min. Tennis Professional: $90/60min or $50/30min for juniors. Assistant Pro: $65/60min or $35/30min for juniors. Semi-private and group rates available.

**Q: Do you have pickleball?**
A: Yes — 4 hard courts. Guests pay $10. List all players on reservation. One court per reservation for up to 120 minutes.
